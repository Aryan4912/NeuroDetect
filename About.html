About NeuroDetect

Empowering early brain tumor detection with AI — for every patient, everywhere.

Who We Are

NeuroDetect is a forward-thinking healthcare initiative built at the intersection of technology, medicine, and compassion. Our team is composed of researchers, engineers, and medical consultants working toward one mission: to improve access to brain tumor diagnostics using AI-powered tools.

What We Do

NeuroDetect uses cutting-edge machine learning to scan and interpret brain CT scans. Our AI system performs three critical tasks: tumor detection, tumor type classification, and generation of a detailed diagnostic report using medically-relevant terminology. This makes it easier for healthcare professionals to act quickly — and for patients to understand their condition with clarity.

Why It Matters

In rural and low-resource settings, waiting for a diagnosis can mean the difference between life and death. With a lack of expert radiologists or neurologists, even a simple CT scan may not be properly analyzed for days. NeuroDetect bridges that gap — providing an instant, intelligent second opinion, powered by deep learning.

Our Social Impact

NeuroDetect empowers not just doctors, but patients — by offering instant, interpretable AI reports that can be shared with physicians even in the absence of specialist radiologists. It’s a step toward healthcare equity powered by intelligent automation.
